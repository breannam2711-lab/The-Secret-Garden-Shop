About The Secret Garden Shop

Discover the story behind our passion for bringing nature's beauty into your home. From humble beginnings to becoming your trusted source for exquisite floral arrangements and garden supplies.

Our History

Founded in 2012 by passionate botanist Anna Browne, The Secret Garden Shop began as a small roadside stand offering fresh-cut flowers from local gardens. What started as a weekend hobby quickly blossomed into a beloved community staple.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include rare exotic plants, artisanal gardening tools, and comprehensive landscaping services. Our commitment to sustainability and supporting local growers has remained at the heart of everything we do.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers across the region, helping them create their own secret gardens and bringing the joy of nature into countless homes and businesses.

Our Mission

To cultivate beauty and connection through nature, providing exceptional floral arrangements and gardening resources that inspire creativity and bring joy to our customers' lives.

Our Vision

To be the premier destination for floral excellence and gardening expertise, fostering a community where everyone can discover the transformative power of nature in their daily lives.
